    Fire Lasted

    After the arrival of several shells in a residential complex, our apartment caught fire. According to eyewitnesses, the apartment was burning for 2 days!

    The tense situation and constant shelling did not allow public utilities to quickly eliminate the fire. Everything acquired over 10 years of a happy life was completely destroyed in an instant!
